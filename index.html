Our Flavour

Welcome to Chhote Nawab – Rourkela's Flavour Destination At Chhote Nawab, we bring you the rich, aromatic tastes of Mughlai and North Indian cuisine, right in the heart of Rourkela. From our signature Dum Biryanis and succulent Kebabs to sizzling Tandoori platters and flavorful Indo-Chinese delights, every dish is crafted with care, tradition, and bold spices. Whether you're dining in with family, celebrating with friends, or ordering from home, Chhote Nawab promises a warm ambiance, generous portions, and a truly royal culinary experience. Come taste the nawabi difference.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To serve authentic, high-quality Mughlai and Indian cuisine with a modern touch—delighting every guest with flavourful dishes, warm hospitality, and a memorable dining experience that celebrates tradition and taste.

Our Vision: To become Rourkela’s most loved dining destination for Mughlai and fusion cuisine—known for consistency, customer satisfaction, and culinary innovation—while expanding our presence and preserving the rich heritage of nawabi flavours.
